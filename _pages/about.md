Welcome! I am a Ph.D. student and Research Assistant at **Marquette University**, working under the supervision of [Dr. Kanchan Chowdhury](https://kanchanchy.github.io/) in the **Database, AI and Spatial Systems Lab**. My research focuses on **Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI)**, including spatial representation learning, geospatial machine learning, and AI-based spatial reasoning. I am interested in developing intelligent methods that help machines better understand geometric and topological relationships in real-world spatial data.


In my free time, I’m a huge <b>Real Madrid</b> fan and love <b>photography</b>—it’s my creative escape.
